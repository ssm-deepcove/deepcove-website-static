Wilmot Lodge

Located at the main area of Deep Cove just after the managers office.

There are 8 x rooms with bunk (2 beds) sleeps x 2 and 1 x room with 2 x bunks (4 Beds) sleeps x 4

Facilities include:
  • A common area with lounge and tv
  • Cooking area with electric oven
  • Showers (Separate male and female shower and toilet units and a
    separate wheelchair accessible toilet/shower unit)
Additional Information:

All beds come with pillow case and bottom sheet only. Bedding and towel additional $10 per bed.

Additional space in our school hostel may be available during school holidays, please contact our bookings team to enquire Bookings@deepcovehostel.co.nz
